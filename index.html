Who we are? Be glad to Share!!!

linkedmeal2U is a platform, where you’ll find millions of people giving away food, all for free. this includes almost expired groceries from supermart, surplus meals from hotels, resturants etc

Share a meal today via our Webapp

Fight waste . Help your neighbours. Save our planet. Combat against hunger!

How to share

1. Take a Photo of the food item. Set the pickup location – home/a safe space, or a public location.

2. Get notified via a call when you have a request.

3. Feel great knowing you’ve helped save the planet and made a neighbour’s day!


How to request

1. Get notified via call when new things are added. See where they are, and view the user’s profile.

2. Send a friendly message requesting the items.

3. Show up at the agreed Location. Accept and be thankful

After decades of steady decline, the number of people who suffer from hunger – as measured by the prevalence of undernourishment – began to slowly increase again in 2015. Current estimates show that nearly 690 million people are hungry, or 8.9 percent of the world population – up by 10 million people in one year and by nearly 60 million in five years. The world is not on track to achieve Zero Hunger by 2030. If recent trends continue, the number of people affected by hunger would surpass 840 million by 2030. According to the World Food Programme, 135 million suffer from acute hunger largely due to man-made conflicts, climate change and economic downturns.

At the same time, a profound change of the global food and agriculture system is needed if we are to nourish the more than 690 million people who are hungry today – and the additional 2 billion people the world will have by 2050. Increasing agricultural productivity and sustainable food production are crucial to help alleviate the perils of hunger.
