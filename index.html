Feed a mouth Share!!!

Share a meal today via our Webapp

linkedmeal2U is a platform, where you’ll find millions of people giving away food, all for free. this includes almost expired groceries from supermart, surplus meals from hotels, resturants etc

Fight waste . Help your neighbours. Save our planet. Combat against hunger!

How to share

1. Take a Photo of the food item. Set the pickup location – home/a safe space, or a public location.

2. Get notified via a call when you have a request.

3. Feel great knowing you’ve helped save the planet and made a neighbour’s day!


How to request

1. Get notified via call when new things are added. See where they are, and view the user’s profile.

2. Send a friendly message requesting the items.

3. Show up at the agreed Location. Accept and be thankful
